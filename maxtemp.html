Max Temperature





As expected and as shown in the plot, the closer a city is to the equator (0 latitude) the warmer the max temperature will be.The farther one is from the equator the lower the max temperature is likely to be in that city. Data points farthest away from the temperature exhibit the coldest temperatures.
